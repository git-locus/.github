## The Locus Manifesto

For too long, innovation has been locked away. Ideas trapped in boardrooms. That era is ending. Because today, the spark of
change can come from anywhere. This is for those crazy enough to believe a kid in their bedroom can launch the next movement.

We are building a community of dreamers and doers reimagining how ideas are born, grown, and launched.

Because locus isn't just a platform. Locus is a whole new era of ideas.

**And you're invited.**

## What you can do on Locus

- 🔭 **Explore**: browse ideas through categories, see what's new or what's catching fire.
- ✍️ **Post**: share an idea in 314 characters, tagged with a category and subcategory.
- 🤝 **Join**: hit Join on a post to say *"I want to help you build this."*
- 💬 **Chat**: talk it through. The next teammate might be one message away.

No vanity metrics. No outrage-optimized feeds. Just a place to think, share, and build together.

## People of Locus

**01 · The Starters**: They don't wait for permission. Half-formed thoughts, messy sketches, late-night notes. Ideas don't
need to be finished to matter — they just need a place to begin.

**02 · The Builders**: They see potential where others see drafts. They join not to own, but to shape. The best ideas are
never built alone.

**03 · The Curious**: Not here to scroll, here to believe. They chase sparks and weird early signals. Every movement starts
 with curiosity.

**04 · The Backers**: They recognize signals early, support with trust, resources, and belief — sometimes before the world
is ready.
